IUH

The Forerunner of the Industrial university of HO CHI MINH University was Professional Tranning School in Go Vap and founded December 1956. March 1999, the school was upgraded to college Industry IV and December 2004 School was up graded to Ho chi Minh University of Industrial under Decision No 214/2004/QD - TTg of the Prime Minister
